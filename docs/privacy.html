SmartStylist

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Summary: SmartStylist stores all your personal data — body profile, clothing items, and outfit history — exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers or collect analytics. The only external services used are Google Gemini AI (for style analysis) and OpenWeather (for real-time weather). Images sent to Gemini are not stored by us or retained beyond the single API call.

1. What data we collect

SmartStylist collects the following information to provide its core features:

  • Physical profile — body type, skin tone, eye colour, hair colour, and the seasonal colorimetry result produced by Gemini AI.
  • Wardrobe items — photographs, categories, primary colour, pattern, style tags, and status (active / archived / disposed) of garments you add.
  • Outfit history — combinations of garments worn on specific dates, along with the occasion and weather context at that time.
  • Location — used transiently to obtain real-time weather for outfit suggestions. Coordinates are never stored beyond the current app session.

2. Local-only storage

Every item in the list above is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData persistence framework. SmartStylist does not have, and has never had, its own backend server or cloud database.

Your data is protected by iOS full-disk encryption. Deleting the app removes all associated data permanently and irreversibly.

3. Google Gemini AI

SmartStylist uses Google Gemini AI for two purposes:

  • Style analysis during onboarding — your profile answers are sent to Gemini to derive a seasonal colorimetry palette and personalised style guidelines.
  • Wardrobe ingestion — when you add a garment, its photograph is sent to Gemini's vision model to extract category, colour, and pattern metadata automatically.
Data sent to Gemini is processed in real time for a single response. SmartStylist does not store your images or profile data on any server. Google's handling of API data is governed by the Gemini API Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

4. OpenWeather API

SmartStylist uses the OpenWeather 3.0 API to fetch real-time conditions based on your device location. Your coordinates are sent to OpenWeather's servers to retrieve temperature, sky conditions, and precipitation likelihood. This data is used solely to contextualise outfit suggestions and is never stored by SmartStylist.

5. Analytics and tracking

SmartStylist does not include any analytics, advertising, A/B testing, crash-reporting, or behavioural-tracking SDKs. There is no Firebase, no Amplitude, no Mixpanel, no Meta SDK, and no App Tracking Transparency prompt because there is nothing to track.

6. Children's privacy

SmartStylist is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has used the app, please use the in-app deletion feature or contact us.

7. Your rights and data deletion

Because all data lives on your device, you have complete control:

  • Navigate to Profile → Delete All My Data within the app to erase every SwiftData record (UserProfile, ClothingItem, OutfitHistory) immediately.
  • Use Profile → Retake Analysis to delete only your style profile while keeping your wardrobe intact.
  • Uninstalling SmartStylist from your device permanently removes all local data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
